Primordia

primordia is a four-page (mostly) pamphlet releasing roughly every two weeks - on the Kalends (1st of the month) and the Ides (13th/15th of the month) or immediately after. Should those dates fall on a date when school is not in session, they will be released on the next possible day on this website.

Only one person is working and authoring this website and each pamphlet, so direct all concerns towards the following Google form here.
